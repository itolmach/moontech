May 1st Strategic Vision

The MoonTech Flywheel

Closing the effort-to-earnings gap by redesigning the emotional arc of the creator experience.

what we have and why it doesn't work

we are expriencing a structural cognitive tax baked into the design. When a fully-engaged user with no distractions takes minutes longer than necessary, the friction isn't coming from interruptions — it's coming from the design forcing unnecessary cognitive work at every decision point.

The UI is making them do work they shouldn't be doing. Each screen in the sequence requires active parsing instead of guided action, and the cognitive debt compounds without a reward release mechanism.

Violated Principle: Cognitive Load & Comprehension

Human processing power is a finite resource. Design must act as a filter, not a floodgate.

  • Terminology Clarity: Eliminate internal jargon. Align with the user's mental model.
  • Information Density: Only surface data required for the immediate decision.
  • Explicit Upfront Requirements: Never hide the "cost" of an action (time, effort, or data).
  • Frictionless Choice Architecture: Use Hick's Law to guide toward the logical next step.

May 1st Sprint Diagnostic

I. The Entropy of 18.6%

Only 930/5,000 ads closed. In a high-integrity system, this 81.4% drop-off represents systemic noise. The creator’s slacking is a rational economic calculation to preserve time.

II. Temporal Incoherence

Moontech lacks the algorithmic enforcement that makes programmatic ads predictable. You cannot promise performance if the execution layer is decoupled from scheduling.

III. Risk & Friction Asymmetry

Requiring scripts and double-uploads for unpaid tasks is a friction multiplier. When a task is speculative, the workflow must be near-zero friction to be viable.

IV. Human-in-the-Loop Debt

Manual intervention is a sign of a broken protocol. Communication should be a function of architecture, not nagging. Chasing creators consumes absolute margin.

V. Epistemic Feedback

The "weekend data blackout" destroys the creator's ability to correlate effort with earnings. Cybernetics requires a tight feedback loop for self-optimization.

The Goal

Produce a single emotional outcome: "This is exactly what I hoped it would be and it was so quick."

Strategic Solutions & Next Steps

1. Guest Mode

Inverts the current trust equation. users discover H&M and Adidas campaigns and potential earnings before any registration prompt. The gate moves to payout, where identity verification is actually required.

2. Link-Only Ad Closure

Meets influencers in their native environment: create, post, paste link, done. The dropout points are removed entirely rather than optimized. This matches their existing mental model exactly.

3. Waitlist + Platform-Ad

Sidesteps the cold start problem by ensuring the platform can always run a campaign for itself. It converts acquisition spend into a live earning experience that validates the promise on day one.
